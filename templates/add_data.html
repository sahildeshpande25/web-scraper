Here's how you can add your data

If you choose the Via Form option, you will have to manually enter all the data fields through a form and submit it. If you choose the Import CSV option, you will be able to upload a CSV of your choice that contains all the fields that the form requests.

Please note that you must first click on View Data so that the data can be fetched from the google spreadsheet and then set up the table in our database.
